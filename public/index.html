💡 About Me

I’m a researcher working at the intersection of Arabic literature and digital humanities, with a focus on grief, memory, and identity in classical and modern Arabic poetry.

My other interests lie in the field of epigraphy — exploring Arabic inscriptions, their placement in architectural contexts, and the layered meanings they create between text, space, and visibility.
